How to Play

1

Describe It

Use as many words as you like to describe the card to your team. No saying the word itself!

2

One Word

Same cards, but now you can only say a single word as a clue. Choose wisely!

3

Act It Out

No words allowed! Use charades to get your team to guess the card.
